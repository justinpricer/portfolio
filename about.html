About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Justin Pricer, and like all of you I've decided to travel down a different path in search of a better life and career. I'm originally from the Rio Grande Valley in South Texas, but after joining the Army in 2009, the military gave me the opportunity to live all over the world. I have an eleven year old daughter who is the center of my world, and a wife who has given me the world. I'm a lucky guy in an unlucky world, but always looking for the bright side and best in everyone around me.
